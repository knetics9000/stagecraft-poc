## JoVE VOCABULARY GLOSSARY (for the AI's system prompt)

- **Recs** — recommendations from faculty or librarians; a key buying signal
- **Champs** — champions; faculty members internally advocating for the purchase
- **Unl** / **Unlimited** — a product tier (mentioned as sunsetting at end of fiscal period)
- **Lib** — library
- **PathoPhys** — Pathophysiology (a JoVE content area)
- **Edu / Res** — Education vs. Research licensing tiers
- **Bio Sci pkg** — Biological Sciences bundle
- **A&P** — Anatomy and Physiology (a JoVE Core content area)
- **JoVE Business** — JoVE's business content vertical
- **Provost** — typical university academic budget approver above the library
- **Stages used:** A — Booking, B — Validation, C — Proposition, Renewal Created, Invoiced, Paid, Closed Lost
- **Sales Motions:** Library Model New Business, Faculty Model New Business, Upgrade, Renewal
